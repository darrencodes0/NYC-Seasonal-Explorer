Staten Island

What's Staten Island?

Events

Special Events that occur during Colder Weather Seasons

Staten Island Comedy Festival

An annual comedy festival featuring stand-up performances by both local and nationally recognized comedians.

Date: November or December (Varies)

Staten Island Zoo's Spooktacular

A family-friendly Halloween event at the Staten Island Zoo featuring themed exhibits, activities, and entertainment.

Date: Late October

Winter Wonderland at Snug Harbor Cultural Center

A festive event featuring holiday lights, decorations, and activities for the whole family to enjoy.

Date: Late November to Early January

Staten Island Film Festival

An annual festival celebrating independent filmmaking, featuring screenings, panels, and workshops.

Date: Fall season, typically in November
